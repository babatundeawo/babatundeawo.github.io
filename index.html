About Me

I am a multidisciplinary STEM educator, atmospheric physicist, and consultant with over a decade of experience bridging scientific research with educational technology. My expertise lies in developing computational models for climate science and implementing robust STEM curricula (Coding & Robotics) across educational institutions in Nigeria.

I hold an M.Sc. in Atmospheric Physics and a B.Sc. in Physics from the University of Ibadan. My work spans atmospheric radiative transfer, remote sensing, renewable energy modeling, data analysis, and STEM education.

My mission is to empower the next generation of Africans with critical STEM skills using visual learning, interactive experiments, and technology-driven pedagogy.
