Parametric Estimation of Direct Irradiance ‚Äì My Master's thesis (Bulletin of the Science Association of Nigeria, 2020) tackled solar radiation data scarcity in West Africa.

  • Objective: Develop an efficient method to estimate all-sky solar radiation using MODIS satellite data.
